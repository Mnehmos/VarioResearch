Output Format Decisions

Date: 2025-04-23

Research Context

This document outlines the decisions made regarding the final output formats for the Operation Foresight project, taking into consideration the target audience and goals of the research.

Logic Primitive: decide | Task ID: adapt_001

Introduction

The selection of appropriate output formats is critical to ensuring that the findings and recommendations from Operation Foresight reach their intended audience effectively. This document outlines the decisions regarding primary and supporting output formats based on audience analysis and communication goals.

Decision on Final Report Format

Based on the target audience of policymakers and senior security executives, the primary output format for the Operation Foresight report will be an Executive Brief.

Decision ID: dec_f2e73cccd94c
Decision Maker: Deep Research Agent

Rationale: This format is explicitly designed for busy senior executives and is also highly suitable for policymakers who require concise, high-level summaries to quickly grasp key findings and recommendations.

Supporting Materials

In addition to the Executive Brief, the following supporting materials will be created to provide more detail and facilitate presentations:

Formal Report (Comprehensive)

A detailed written report containing all the analysis, data, and sources. This will serve as the foundational document from which the Executive Brief and presentation slides are derived.

Purpose: To provide complete documentation of the research process, findings, and recommendations for stakeholders who require in-depth understanding.

Primary Users: Research teams, technical specialists, implementation teams

Conclusion

This combination of outputs ensures that the core message is accessible and impactful for senior decision-makers (Executive Brief), while providing the necessary depth and supporting information for those who require it (Formal Report) and materials for communicating the findings in a presentation setting (Presentation Slides).

The three-tiered approach maximizes the impact and utility of the Operation Foresight research by catering to different stakeholder needs and usage scenarios.
